Product Overview
CPDU Series — Intelligent Rack PDUs
Single-phase and three-phase IPDU smart meter products built on PDUMind's innovative SUM (Sustainable, Upgradable, Maintainable) design concept. Active metering for energy optimisation and circuit protection with user-programmable alarm thresholds — real-time local and remote alerts for potential circuit overloads.
AI Clusters HPC Hyper-scale DC Colocation IDC Finance & Banking Government Enterprise
Key Features
01
Energy-Efficient Latching Relays
Bi-stable latching relays consume up to 70% less energy than alternatives. In case of PDU failure, power distribution to the rack is maintained.
02
PDU Cascading — Up to 16 Units
Daisy-chain up to 16 PDUs from a single IP address via Cat5/6 cable, dramatically reducing network port requirements in large deployments.
03
±1% Billing-Grade kWh Accuracy
Enables precise allocation of data center energy expenses with billing-grade ±1% metering accuracy per outlet or phase.
04
IEX Universal Combo Socket
UL and IEC tested C13/C19 combo socket accepts C14 or C20 plugs. Alternating phase on 3-phase systems helps balance load across branches.
05
Dual Gigabit Ethernet Ports
World's first dual GbE port PDU. Share monitoring between data center management systems and end clients across two separate networks.
06
Advanced Security
256-bit AES encryption · RADIUS · SNMP v3 · SSHv2 · SSL · HTTPS. Supports the most stringent data center security requirements.
07
RCM Residual Current Monitoring
Optional BENDER RCM module measures ground wire current, reducing risk of electric shock and simplifying regulatory auditing.
08
Hydraulic-Magnetic Circuit Breakers
Not affected by ambient temperature. Proactively monitors breaker status and sends overload alerts before a tripped circuit causes downtime.

3-Phase Distribution & Monitoring PDUs
CM-V Series
The CM-V series are vertical rack-mount, 3-phase intelligent distribution PDUs built from 1U/1.4U aluminum extrusion profiles. Each unit ships with a 3-metre IEC60309 industrial power cord and integrates a hot-swappable IPDU meter head for total detection of voltage, current, power, and energy consumption. Hydraulic-magnetic circuit breakers provide IT-grade overload protection on every branch.
